Rules:
- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds, the 1st to reach the target score wins!
- In each turn, a player rolls 2 dices as many times as he whishes. The result of both dices will get added to his current score
- BUT, if any of the player's dices rolls a 1, his current score will be lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The player can click 'Hold' which will transfer your current score into your total score ending your round.
- I the sum of a player's dices equal to 6 twice in a row, the player will loose both his current and Total score.
- The target score can be changed at any time.
